Balancing of multiple mass in single plane

Post Test

Q1. Balancing of multiple mass in single plane the net force and moment should be-
A   Net force > net moment
B   Net force = 0, net moment ≠ 0
C   Net moment > net force
D   Net force = 0, net moment = 0
Ans is D


Q2. For dynamic balancing of a shaft,
A   the net dynamic force acting on the shaft is equal to zero
B   the net couple due to dynamic forces acting on the shaft is equal to zero
C   both (A) and (B)
D   none of the above
Ans is C


Q3. A disturbing mass m1 attached to a rotating shaft may be balanced by a single mass m2 attached in same plane of rotation as that of m1 such that
A   m1.r2 =m2.r1
B   m1. r1 = m2.r2
C   m 2.m1 = r1.r2
D   none of the above
Ans is B


Q4. For a balanced system force polygon should exist.
A   false
B   true
Ans is B


Q5. Four masses m1, m2, m3and m4are 200 kg, 300 kg, 240 kg and 260kgrespectively. The corresponding radii of rotation are 0.2 m, 0.15 m, 0.25 m and0.3m respectively and the angles between successive masses are 45°, 75° and135°.Find the position and magnitude of the balance mass required, if its radius of rotation is 0.2 m.
A   116 kg and 201.48°
B   111 kg and 201.48°
C   100 kg and 210.48°
D   116 kg and 210.48°
Ans is A
