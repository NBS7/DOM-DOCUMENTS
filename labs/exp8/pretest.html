Disc type flywheel

Pre Test

Q1. What is the value of the radius of gyration of disc type flywheel as compared to a rim type flywheel for the same diameter?
A   \(\sqrt{2}\) times
B   \(\frac{1}{2}\)times
C   2 times
D   \(\frac{1}{\sqrt {2}}\) times
Ans is D


Q2. In the case of a flywheel, the maximum fluctuation energy is the
A   ratio of the maximum and minimum energy
B   ratio of the minimum and maximum energy
C   difference between the maximum and minimum energies
D   sum of maximum and minimum energies
Ans is C


Q3. What is the moment of inertia of disc type of flywheel?
A   I= Mr2
B   I=0.5*Mr2
C   I=2*Mr2
D   I=0.4*Mr2
Ans is B


Q4. What is the value of the radius of gyration of the disc type flywheel as compared to rim type flywheel for the same diameter?

Statement A: To absorb energy when demand of energy id less than the supply

Statement B: To give out energy when demand of energy is more than the supply


A   \(\frac{21}{2}\) times
B   \(\frac{1}{\frac{21}{2}}\) times
C   2 times
D   \(\frac{1}{2}\) times
Ans is B
